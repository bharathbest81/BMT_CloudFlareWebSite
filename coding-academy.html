Frequently Asked Questions

What age can my child start?

Children can start as early as age 6. Our Logic & Problem Solving course uses visual block-based coding which is perfect for young beginners who are still developing reading and typing skills.

Do I need to buy any special equipment?

No special equipment is required! A standard laptop or desktop computer (Windows or Mac) with an internet connection is all you need. We use browser-based tools and free software for our courses.

Are classes online or in-person?

We offer both! You can choose fully online, interactive sessions via Zoom/Meet, or in-person classes if you are located near our New Zealand training centres.

What if my child has no experience at all?

That's completely fine! Our beginner courses assume zero prior knowledge. We start from the very basics and build up at a pace comfortable for every student.

Will my child get a certificate?

Yes, students receive a certificate of completion at the end of each term/course, highlighting the specific skills they've mastered and projects they've built.
