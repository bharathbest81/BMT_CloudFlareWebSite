FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development services.

How long does a website take to build?
Timelines vary by complexity. A brochure site typically takes 2–3 weeks; e-commerce or custom web apps may take 6–10 weeks. We'll give you a clear project timeline upfront during our discovery call.
Do you provide SEO with every website?
Yes — SEO-optimised architecture, meta tags, structured data, and page speed are built into every project from day one. We don't treat SEO as an add-on; it's part of our core development process.
Will my website work on mobile?
Absolutely. We build mobile-first — every site is fully responsive and tested across a wide range of devices and screen sizes before launch. Mobile users are always our primary consideration.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes — we offer full redesigns and can migrate content from your current platform. Whether you're on WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, or a custom-built site, we can modernise it without losing your existing content or SEO rankings.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes — we offer flexible monthly support and maintenance packages after launch. This includes plugin updates, security monitoring, content updates, performance checks, and priority bug fixes.
